Cheesecake with blueberry jam

Necessary Products Biscuits 1 package, Butter 1 packet(125g), Cream cheese 2 packages (250g), Sugar 50-60g, Vanilia 1 packet (2g), Sour creame 500g, Powdered sugar 2 tbps., Blueberries 200g. jam, Method of preparation: Grind the biscuits into flour and mix with the melted butter.The mixture is distributed on the bottom of the mold and left in the refrigerator.Sugar, vanilia and cream,whipped with powdered sugar,are added to the whipped cheese.All ingredients are beaten until a uniform cream is obtained. The cooled cake is covered with the cream and returned to the refrigerator.The cake is spread with the jam.Then leave the cheesecake in the fridge overnight.

HEALTH TIPS

Mix bananas, honey, and water Ingredients: 450 ml boiling water 2 medium ripe bananas with spots 2 tablespoons, honey PREPARATION: 1. First, peel the bananas and puree them with a wooden fork or spoon. Tip: Do not use metal cutlery as the bananas tend to darken when they come into contact with metal. 2. Then, put the bananas in the pot and pour boiled water over them. Let the mixture steep for 25 to 30 minutes. 3. Finally, when the remedy has cooled, add honey. You can strain the mixture if you prefer it transparent. Important: Add the honey last so that it does not lose its important properties through the boiling water. CONSUMPTION: Drink 150 ml of this remedy 3 times a day (450 ml in total per day). Important: Add the honey last so that it does not lose its important properties through the boiling water. This amount is enough for one day and you can make a fresh one the next morning. First results come in 5 days.
